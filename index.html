Flora
Amazing Flora

Throughout its entire territory there is a great variety of ecosystems, this richness makes it the seventh country in the world with the greatest biodiversity. An estimated calculus of the flora goes for thirty thosend different especies in the country.

Culture
Rich culture

Venezuela is a country where multiple cultures and races coexist in harmony. It has a very rich indigenous cultural heritage and since ships from all over the world arrived in colonial times, it has African and European cultural heritage also.

Fauna
Fascinating Fauna

There are many species of primates found in Venezuela, but the most common is the howler monkey. Just as there are big cats like the puma or the jaguar. There is also, the capybara, considered the largest rodent in the world inhabiting the shores of the Orinoco.

Food
Delicius Food

You can taste the national dish called Pabellon made of rice, black beans, fried ripe plantane and beef in threads. There is also the international famous Arepa with the an incredible variaty of fills and names.

Nature
Breathtaking Nature

South of Venezuela begins the Amazon rainforest, the largest rainforest and the largest nature reserve in the world. This area of the country has great touristic value, because there is the Angel Falls, the highest waterfall of the world; savannah, rivers, forests and tepuis.

Fun Facts

As for bird species, it is estimated that there are approximately 1,364, among them 48 are part of the endemic fauna of Venezuela. The national animal of Venezuela is the turpial, a bird with beautiful black and yellow or orange plumage.
